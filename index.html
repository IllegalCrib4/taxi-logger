    How to use Taxi Logger

    Welcome to the ultimate taxi dispatch system! Here's how to run your service:

    1
    Set the Stage: Select your server first (e.g., "Cherno 2", "Pripyat" or "LUX"). The system will then automatically load the correct towns and POIs for that map.
    2
    Log the Fare: Enter the pickup, dropoff, and player names. Hit Generate Trip to format the details for dispatch.
    3
    Dispatch to Discord: Click Copy to Clipboard in the Output module, then paste it directly into your Discord taxi channel. 🚕💨
    4
    Broadcast In-Game: Copy status updates from the In Game Ads section, then paste them directly into the in-game chat to alert survivors.
    5
    Track Progress: Your trips are automatically logged to the history and stats panel, so you can track your earnings and activity.
